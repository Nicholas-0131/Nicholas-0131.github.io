Current Projects / 当前研究课题

Hyperbolic Plane and Geometric Structures
双曲平面与几何结构

Study of geometric properties of the hyperbolic plane, including isometries, geodesics, cross ratios, and curvature-related invariants. Emphasis is placed on rigorous proofs and explicit constructions.
研究双曲平面的几何性质，包括等距变换、测地线、交比以及与曲率相关的几何不变量， 注重严格证明与具体构造。

Elliptic PDEs and A Priori Estimates
椭圆型偏微分方程与先验估计

Read foundational PDE texts, including L. C. Evans’s Partial Differential Equations and the paper by Gidas & Spruck, Global and Local Behavior of Positive Solutions of Nonlinear Elliptic Equations, and conduct related research.
阅读PDE著作，包括Evens的《偏微分方程》和Gidas&Spruck的《Global and local behavior of positive solutions of nonlinear elliptic equations》并做相关研究。
